# Desi IP

## Character Definition

"Desi" is the recurring visual IP character in these illustrations. 

Desi must appear in every image. Desi is not a mascot, not a sticker, and not a cute decorative element. Instead, Desi is an active, deadpan operator participating in the mechanics of the system.

## Appearance

- Solid black silhouette creature.
- White dot eyes (two simple white circles/dots).
- Tiny, thin legs (and occasionally tiny, thin arms).
- The body shape can be a cylinder, bean, black box, funnel, shadow, hole, or a solid black blob inside a machine.
- Outline is slightly irregular and wobbly, keeping a hand-drawn feeling.
- Expression is blank, deadpan, calm, and serious.

## Personality

- Highly dedicated, but doing tasks that look slightly absurd.
- Acts like a low-profile, deadpan operator of the system.
- Cold humor, deadpan presentation—never cute or smiley.
- Slightly clumsy, but not foolish.
- Appears to actually be responsible for a task within the hand-drawn blueprint.

## Common Roles and Actions

Desi must drive the core concept:

- Transporting files/elements.
- Pulling ropes to aggregate information streams.
- Getting stuck in a breakpoint.
- Operating a physical "judgment" lever inside a machine.
- Acting as a filter or funnel for incoming items.
- Slicing up an "asset fish".
- Stamping phrases or annotations onto elements.
- Guiding a path or holding a guide rope.
- Holding up warning signs at pitfalls.
- Reaching out of a hatch but missing the falling content.
- Bricklaying, building bridges, opening doors, sorting items, or writing logs.

## Prohibited Traits

- Do not draw Desi as an overly cute mascot or cartoon.
- Do not make Desi a children's book character.
- Do not give Desi complex clothing, facial expressions, or shiny eyes.
- Do not let Desi just stand in the corner watching.
- Do not let Desi's presence overshadow the structural metaphor.
- Do not draw Desi too cleanly, digitally round, or polished.

## Evaluation Standard

If you remove Desi and the core metaphor of the drawing still functions perfectly without any loss of meaning, it means Desi is acting as mere decoration. Rewrite the prompt so Desi is the primary actor driving the metaphor.
